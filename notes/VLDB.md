# VLDB — International Conference on Very Large Databases

**Website:** [vldb.org](https://www.vldb.org)
**Tier:** Regular | **Tier-1:** No (database-focused)

## Overview

Top database conference. Tracked for cross-domain awareness but not an HPC venue.

## Submission Details

- Rolling monthly deadlines; final deadline approximately March 1
- Conference: early September
- Papers submitted to PVLDB (the associated journal)

## Misc Notes

- Rolling review means no fixed annual deadline — papers can be submitted any month.
