# HPCA — IEEE International Symposium on High Performance Computer Architecture

**Website:** [hpca-conf.org](https://hpca-conf.org)
**Tier:** Top | **Tier-1:** Yes

## Overview

Top venue for computer architecture research with a focus on high-performance systems. Held annually in February–March, often co-located with PPoPP and CGO.

## Submission Details

- Paper deadline: approximately August of the prior year (dates approximate — verify each year)
- Conference: approximately February–March
- Double-blind review

## Review Process

- Double-blind. Competitive acceptance rate (~20%).

## Misc Notes

- Dates in this tracker are approximate. Verify at the HPCA CFP page before planning submissions.
- Often held the same week as PPoPP and CGO.
