# ICS — International Conference on Supercomputing

**Website:** [dipsa-qub.github.io/ICS2026-webpage](https://dipsa-qub.github.io/ICS2026-webpage/)
**Tier:** Regular | **Tier-1:** Yes

## Overview

ACM conference on supercomputing and high-performance computing systems. Held annually in mid-July.

## Submission Details

- **Two submission cycles:**
  - Cycle 1 deadline: December 16 of the prior year
  - Cycle 2 deadline: February 9
- Conference: mid-July
- Double-blind review

## Review Process

- Papers rejected from Cycle 1 may be revised and resubmitted for Cycle 2.

## Misc Notes

- The two-cycle system provides flexibility: an early December submission gives time to revise and try again in February if needed.
