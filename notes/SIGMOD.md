# SIGMOD — ACM Special Interest Group on Management of Data

**Website:** [2026.sigmod.org](https://2026.sigmod.org)
**Tier:** Regular | **Tier-1:** No (not HPC-focused; kept for cross-domain tracking)

## Overview

Top venue for data management research. Not HPC-focused but tracked for cross-domain awareness.

## Submission Details

- **Four rounds per year:** deadlines roughly January 17 / April 17 / July 17 / October 17
- Conference: mid-June
- Double-blind review

## Misc Notes

- Rolling round system means you can submit up to 4 times per year.
