# DRBSD — International Workshop on Data Analysis and Reduction for Big Scientific Data

**Website:** [drbsd.github.io](https://drbsd.github.io/)
**Tier:** Workshop | **Tier-1:** No

## Overview

Workshop co-located with SC (Supercomputing), focused on data reduction and analysis for scientific computing. A good venue for early-stage work in data compression and reduction for HPC.

## Submission Details

- Paper deadline: approximately August 15
- Workshop: mid-November (during SC week)

## Misc Notes

- Attending DRBSD requires SC registration (or a workshop-only registration).
- Good place to present early or exploratory work in scientific data reduction.
