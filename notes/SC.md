# SC — Supercomputing

**Website:** [sc26.supercomputing.org](https://sc26.supercomputing.org)
**Tier:** Top | **Tier-1:** Yes

## Overview

IEEE/ACM International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage, and Analysis. The flagship HPC conference. Held annually in mid-November, typically in a major US city.

## Submission Details

- Paper deadline: typically early April (2026: April 8)
- Conference: mid-November
- Double-blind review
- 11-page limit (including references) in ACM format

## Review Process

- Papers go through a rigorous double-blind review process.
- Author rebuttals are included in the process.

## Misc Notes

- **SIGHPC Travel Grants:** Application window is roughly early August to early September. Worth applying for students.
- **Student Volunteers:** Applications typically open in February. Volunteers get free conference registration.
- Co-located workshops include DRBSD and others — check the SC workshops page for the full list.
