# IPDPS — IEEE International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium

**Website:** [ipdps.org](https://www.ipdps.org)
**Tier:** Top | **Tier-1:** Yes

## Overview

One of the top venues for parallel and distributed computing research. Held annually in late May. Sponsored by IEEE.

## Submission Details

- Paper deadline: typically early October of the prior year (2026: October 9, 2025)
- Conference: late May
- Double-blind review
- 10-page limit in IEEE format

## Review Process

- Standard double-blind peer review.
- Competitive acceptance rate (~20–25%).

## Misc Notes

- Co-located with several workshops; check the IPDPS workshops page.
- Good venue for systems-level HPC work, parallel algorithms, and distributed systems.
