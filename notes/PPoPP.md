# PPoPP — Principles and Practice of Parallel Programming

**Website:** [ppopp26.sigplan.org](https://ppopp26.sigplan.org)
**Tier:** Top | **Tier-1:** Yes

## Overview

ACM SIGPLAN symposium focused on the principles and practice of parallel programming — including programming models, languages, runtime systems, and performance. Held annually in late January, often co-located with CGO and HPCA.

## Submission Details

- Paper deadline: typically September 1 of the prior year
- Conference: late January
- Double-blind review
- Page limit varies; check the current CFP

## Review Process

- Double-blind. Competitive acceptance rate (~20%).

## Misc Notes

- Often co-located with CGO and HPCA in the same week — good opportunity to attend multiple venues.
- Strong focus on programming languages and runtime systems for parallel architectures.
