# ICDE — IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering

**Website:** [icde2026.github.io](https://icde2026.github.io/)
**Tier:** Regular | **Tier-1:** No (data engineering focused)

## Overview

IEEE conference on data engineering. Not HPC-focused but tracked for cross-domain awareness.

## Submission Details

- **Two submission rounds:**
  - Round 1 deadline: approximately June 18 of the prior year
  - Round 2 deadline: approximately October 27 of the prior year
- Conference: early May

## Misc Notes

- Two-round system gives flexibility. Round 1 rejections can be revised for Round 2.
